Cognitive Functions in Depth

The eight Jungian functions are combinations of perception and judgment modes, expressed through introversion or extraversion.

  • Introverted Thinking (Ti) – internal logic and analysis
  • Extraverted Intuition (Ne) – idea generation and possibilities
  • Introverted Feeling (Fi) – internal values and authenticity
  • Extraverted Sensing (Se) – in-the-moment awareness

These functions influence personality dynamics and behavior across MBTI types.
